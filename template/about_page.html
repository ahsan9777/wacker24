The idea

Purchase of the first company vehicle - Renault R4 "The Delivery Blitz". Technical highlights: 26 HP = 19 kW, displacement: 850cc, four-cylinder in-line engine, length 3.66m, width 1.43m, height 1.47m, empty weight: 600kg, body: high roof station wagon. The then striking red car was in constant use for quickly carrying out repairs to typewriters, printing machines, copiers, overhead projectors and dictation machines on site and for delivering office supplies.
